### Ingredients
- 1 Cup <a href="https://www.walmart.com/grocery/ip/Rice-Select-Jasmati-Rice-32-Oz/10403511" target="_blank">Jasmati Rice</a>
- 1 1/2 Cup Water
- 1/2 Lime
- 1/2 Cup Fresh Cilatro
- 1 Tbsp Vegatable Oil
- Pinch of Salt

### Instructions
1. Bring water to boil.
2. Add rice and return to boil.
3. Heat over low heat for 12-15 minutes; until rice is tender.
4. Chop cilantro if desired; add to the rice.
5. Squeeze lime juice into the rice. (If using lime juice from bottom, add to taste).
6. Add vegetable oil and salt to rice.
7. Stir ingredients.
8. Enjoy!

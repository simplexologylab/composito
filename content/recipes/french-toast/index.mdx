### Ingredients
- 6-8 Slices of Bread
- 4 Eggs
- 2/3 Cup Milk
- 2 Tbsp Sugar
- 1/2 Tsp Vanilla
- Dash Salt
- Dash Cinnamon

### Instructions
1. In a shallow bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, sugar, vanilla, salt and cinnamon until well blended.
2. Heat skillet.
3. Coat bread with mixture until saturated, not soggy.
4. Allow excess to drip off.
5. Place bread onto the hot skillet.
6. Cook until the underside is golden brown, then flip and cook other side.
7. Top with your favorite toppings (syrup, confectioners sugar, fruit).
8. Enjoy

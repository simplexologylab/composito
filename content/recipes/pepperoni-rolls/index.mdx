### Ingredients
- Bread loaves/rolls; our favorite is https://rhodesbakenserv.com/product/white-bread/
- Thin Sliced Pepporini
- Part-skim Mozarella Cheese

### Instructions
1. Thaw bread, usually takes 6-8 hours.
2. Once thawed, set oven to 350 degrees.
3. Spray counter with olive oil spray to keep dough from sticking. 
4. Strench and spread dough as thin as possible without ripping it.
5. Along bottom edge, place a row of pepperoni's. 
6. Overlapping the pepperoni, add a row of cheese.
7. Leave plain or add any special seasonings; totally up to you.
8. Roll to form a cylinder of goodness.
9. Bake in the oven until the bottom of the roll starts to turn golden brown. (Alternative, throw on the grill ðŸ¤¯)

11. Enjoy!
